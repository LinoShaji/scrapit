# ScrapIt

**ScrapIt** is a mobile application designed to streamline the process of scrap management by acting as a middleman between households and scrap dealers. The app enables users to easily connect with local scrap collectors and responsibly dispose of unwanted materials like metal, plastic, electronics, and paper. By simplifying this process, ScrapIt not only helps declutter homes but also contributes to recycling efforts and environmental sustainability.

## Key Features:
- **Easy Scrap Scheduling**: Users can request a pickup from registered scrap dealers in their area at their convenience.
- **Real-Time Price Tracking**: The app provides current scrap rates for different materials, ensuring users get fair prices for their items.
- **Dealer Management**: Scrap dealers can register on the platform, manage requests, and connect with customers in real-time.
- **Geolocation Services**: Users and dealers can track each otherâ€™s locations, optimizing the scrap collection process.
- **Secure Payments**: Built-in payment options allow users to receive payments directly through the app once their scrap has been collected and valued.
- **Sustainability Impact**: Every transaction highlights the environmental impact of recycling the collected materials.

## Tech Stack:
- **Front-End**: Built using Flutter to deliver a smooth cross-platform experience on both Android and iOS.
- **Back-End**: Powered by [Hasura](https://hasura.io/) for real-time GraphQL APIs, enabling rapid backend development and efficient data handling.
- **Database**: PostgreSQL, seamlessly integrated with Hasura for managing user, dealer, and transaction data.
- **Geolocation API**: Integrated for real-time tracking and pickup scheduling.

## Purpose:
ScrapIt aims to modernize and facilitate the scrap disposal process while promoting sustainability. Whether you are a household looking to declutter or a scrap dealer seeking new customers, ScrapIt provides a user-friendly platform to make the entire experience seamless.
